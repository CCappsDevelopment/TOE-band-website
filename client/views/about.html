ABOUT




Throne Of Eden is a high-energy rock n’ roll band based in Jasper, Alabama, comprised of members Justin Harris, Kyle Stough, Ian Smith, and Reagan Hall. Throne Of Eden started as an all original music act, but later added a versatile set of rock-style covers sure to please any crowd! 40+ cover songs along with multiple originals sprinkled in between enables us to entertain various crowds for hours at a time. With professional sound and light production and a versatile set list, Throne Of Eden is sure to appeal to people of all ages, who are fans of all genres. Styles include rock, classic rock, alternative, punk, pop, country, rap and more. They brought forth a sound unique to the modern rock scene with their upcoming debut album, providing harmonies of two  shredding guitarists and a remarkably tight rhythm section. Since their debut EP’s release in July 2014, “...And No One Knows” has had all three tracks (as well as their single, “House of Fate”) played on 80+ international stations, including a stint as the #1 artist on XRP Radio’s Unsigned & Independent charts. With features already in Stencil Magazine, Jamsphere Magazine, New Transcendence, Kill the Music, and more, the band has big plans for the future and hope to see you all along for the ride.
